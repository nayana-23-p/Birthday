My Dearest,

I'm writing to you from a place you haven't reached yet—from the countless tomorrows we'll share, from the laughter that will echo through rooms we haven't entered, from moments that haven't been born but already shimmer with the promise of joy.

Today, as you celebrate another year of being the extraordinary person you are, I want to thank you for all the memories we haven't made yet. Thank you for the sunrise conversations that will warm our souls on cold mornings. Thank you for the spontaneous adventures that will teach us to dance in the rain of uncertainty. Thank you for the quiet evenings where silence will speak louder than words ever could.

I'm grateful for the tears we'll share—both the ones of overwhelming joy and the ones that will teach us strength. Thank you for holding my hand through storms I can't yet see, and for letting me hold yours when the weight of the world feels too heavy.
